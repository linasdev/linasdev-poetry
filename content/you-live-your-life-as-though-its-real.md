```
You live your life as though it's real.
And all ur heartbreak within you just want to seal...
You do believe, about an afterlife.
An afterlife on earth.
And then you say to me:
"I want to be your wife"
```
<!-- more -->
```

I say "yes, please".
And then you use your naked body just to tease.
I accept your fun proposal.
I then drive by a sign that just says "free disposal".
I stop there for a second,
I'll drop of everything, that doesn't please you.
I just recon.

And now that we are married,
And after all the stones we carried.
Will I finally believe that this world is real to me as well.
And even though there is no hell,
I am in heaven, every second I'm with you.
```
