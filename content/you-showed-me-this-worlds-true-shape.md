```
You showed me this world's true shape.
And even tho I am a simple ape,
You wouldn't be able to guess...
But I digress,
But I digress.
```
<!-- more -->
```

You wouldn't be able to guess,
What I have been able to bless.
To bless with humanly qualities,
To be curious about the anomalies.
But I digress,
But I digress.

Will you be able to give me more?
Or will my request for more you ignore?
I'm left, in the end, with many emotions.
And I would explore with you till the end of the oceans.
But I digress,
But I digress.
```
